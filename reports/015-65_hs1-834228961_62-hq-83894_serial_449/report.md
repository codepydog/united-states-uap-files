## 為什麼一本飛碟雜誌會被 FBI 上呈到 Hoover 桌上

Serial 449 起點是 Philadelphia 的訂閱者 Jarvis H. Cooper（401 North Broad Street）跟 FBI Philadelphia Division 通報：他訂的雜誌《Flying Saucers International》Issue 24（1966-07）裡，第 2-3 頁有一篇文章他「認為在闡述 Communist Party 路線」。Cooper 跟 IRS（國稅局）有關係（FBI 信件明寫他是 IRS 職員），這個身分讓他的舉報得到比一般民眾更高的處理層級。

Philadelphia FBI 1966-09-22 把雜誌轉給 Los Angeles Division（雜誌總部 2004 N. Hoover Street, LA）。LA SAC 1966-10-03 把雜誌完整上呈 Director（J. Edgar Hoover）。LA SAC 同時報告：

- LA Division 索引中沒有「RENAUD」相關資料
- 從未對 Amalgamated Flying Saucer Clubs of America 做過調查
- 不打算進一步行動（No further action is contemplated）

FBI 把整本雜誌歸進 62-HQ-83894 主案卷 Serial 449，留下完整影本但不開新案。這跟 [Serial 403](../013-65_hs1-834228961_62-hq-83894_serial_403/report.md) 的處理一樣：把民間出版物整本歸檔。

## §2 LA SAC 上呈函：10/3/66

![page-02](page-02.jpg)

第二頁是 LA SAC 給 Director, FBI 的 1966-10-03 上呈函。完整內容：

> DIRECTOR, FBI
> SAC, LOS ANGELES (100-24442-40/)
>
> FLYING SAUCERS INTERNATIONAL,
> OFFICIAL JOURNAL OF THE
> AMALGAMATED FLYING SAUCER
> CLUBS OF AMERICA, INC.
> IS - MISCELLANEOUS
>
> The Philadelphia Division by letter dated 9/22/66, furnished the Los Angeles Division with Issue No. 24 dated July, 1966, entitled "FLYING SAUCERS INTERNATIONAL", which is the official journal of the Amalgamated Flying Saucer Clubs of America, Inc. The Philadelphia Division received the magazine from Mr. JARVIS H. COOPER, IRS, 401 North Broad Street, Philadelphia, Pennsylvania on 9/19/66. Mr. COOPER stated he subscribed to the magazine because his son had exhibited an interest in flying saucers and outer space. He said that on pages 2 and 3 of Issue No. 24 was an article which he believed expounded the Communist Party (CP) line.
>
> 1966-09-22 費城分局送來 1966 年 7 月號的 Issue No. 24《FLYING SAUCERS INTERNATIONAL》，這是 Amalgamated Flying Saucer Clubs of America, Inc. 的官方刊物。費城分局 1966-09-19 從 IRS 的 Jarvis H. Cooper（賓州費城北 Broad 街 401 號）那裡收到雜誌。Cooper 說他訂這本雜誌是因為他兒子對飛碟和外太空有興趣，他在 Issue 24 第 2-3 頁看到一篇文章，他認為是在闡述 Communist Party 路線。
>
> For the information of the Bureau, the International Headquarters of the Amalgamated Flying Saucer Clubs of America, Inc. is located at 2004 North Hoover Street, Los Angeles, California. The article, on pages 2 and 3, were allegedly written by Master KALEN-LI RENAN, Head of the planet KORENDOR, which was received on 5/4/66 by special directional short-wave radio by BOB RENAUD.
>
> 該組織總部位於洛杉磯 2004 North Hoover Street。第 2-3 頁的文章據稱由「KORENDOR 行星」首領「Master KALEN-LI RENAN」所寫，1966-05-04 由 BOB RENAUD 透過特殊定向短波無線電接收。
>
> The indices of the Los Angeles Division contain no information identifiable with RENAUD, and no investigation has ever been conducted on captioned organization.
>
> No further action is contemplated in this matter by the Los Angeles Office, UACB.
>
> The foregoing is furnished to the Bureau for information.

UACB = Unless Advised to the Contrary by the Bureau（除非局裡另有指示）。LA SAC 對這份雜誌的處置態度是「歸檔不處理」，把判斷權交回給 Hoover 局裡。

## §3 雜誌封面：飛碟懸停在森林上方

![page-03](page-03.jpg)

第三頁是雜誌封面：

- 上方標題大字「**FLYING SAUCERS INTERNATIONAL**」
- 副標：「Official Journal of the Amalgamated Flying Saucer Clubs of America, Inc.」
- 主視覺：一張黑白飛碟照片，碟身有水平條紋（土星環式），懸停在松林上方
- 底部：「SPECIAL AFSCA THIRD NATIONAL FLYING SAUCER CONVENTION ISSUE」
- 「ISSUE NO. 24 / JULY, 1966 / 50¢」

封面照片的飛碟外型跟 1966 年 4 月 Michigan UFO flap（後來 Project Blue Book 評估為「沼氣」結論）流行的「土星環式碟」一致。AFSCA = Amalgamated Flying Saucer Clubs of America，1959 年由 Gabriel Green 在 LA 創立的全國性 UFO 組織。

## §4 Cooper 標記為「CP line」的爭議文章（第 2 頁）

![page-04](page-04.jpg)

第四頁是雜誌內頁第 2 頁：「BRIGHT WORLD IN HANDS OF SAUCER MOVEMENT」（編者按 + 「SPECIAL AFSCA CONVENTION MESSAGE」）。

從畫面隱約可辨的內容片段：

> Make no mistake. Your government is not in the White House or the Congress. IT IS IN THE PENTAGON, AND IN THE HEADQUARTERS OF THE VAST "DEFENSE"-ORIENTED INDUSTRIES. It is THEY, then, who are the masters of war, of poverty, of disease, of the United States. The Saucer movement is currently in the unique position of alerting other nations of expansion of military strategy by the computer which controls the United States.
>
> 別搞錯，你們的政府不在白宮或國會。它在五角大廈，在巨大的「國防」導向工業總部。是「他們」，才是戰爭、貧窮、疾病、美國的主人。飛碟運動目前處於獨特位置，可以警告其他國家美國軍事戰略由電腦所控制。

> Examine the various world organizations now, those who deal with this earth's heavy work. You will see them all to all various viewpoints. You and they have a lot to think about. There were no facts ever even noted but a great mass of statements. Nobody knows what they said, but it can be quite cleanly stated. It is the same as if we'd have a system... It is the future generation that must put it into the law and into action. It is even possible to be made law against the use of the Military Industrial Complex...

文章具體的指控包括：實際的政府在「五角大廈 + 軍事工業綜合體」、軍方策略由「電腦」執行、飛碟運動是警告全球的窗口。Cooper 把這個論述讀成「Communist Party line」（譴責美國軍工複合體 + 把國家權力歸給軍方）的左派批判語言。

文章末尾標明寄送者 SACERS INTERNATIONAL 在 LA 的地址。

## §19 為什麼 IRS 職員的舉報得到 FBI 認真處理

對照 [Section 9](../008-65_hs1-834228961_62-hq-83894_section_9/report.md)：1957 Sputnik 後 FBI 已經把 UFO 雜誌 + 民間出版品歸進主案卷的習慣，逐漸從個案調查轉向「資訊監控」。Serial 449 是 1966 年這個趨勢的典型範例：

1. **舉報人身分**：Jarvis H. Cooper 是 IRS（國稅局）員工。聯邦員工檢舉同類聯邦敏感議題會優先處理
2. **內容定性**：Cooper 沒舉報「飛碟現象」，而是「Communist Party line」。1966 年越戰升溫期，反美軍工複合體論述會被當作親共指標
3. **跨州轉案**：Philadelphia 收件 → Los Angeles 轉案（因雜誌總部在 LA）→ 上呈 Director。三層處理顯示 FBI 內部的 misclassification miscellaneous 機制
4. **歸檔不調查**：LA SAC 明寫 No further action contemplated。把資料保存下來，但不開新案

LA SAC 信件中的「100-24442-40/」是 LA Division 內部子案編號（100 系列 = Subversive Matters，是 FBI 的反顛覆檔案分類）。但 Bureau 最終把它歸進 62 系列 Internal Security 主案卷（Serial 449），意味 Hoover 認為這是 UFO 案件而不是反顛覆案件。
